## Overview

ReplyMagic helps creators, coaches, ecommerce brands, agencies, and social teams keep up with Instagram comments — without sounding like a bot.

It studies your captions, recent replies, and the visual context of each post, then drafts responses in your voice. Every reply lands in an approval queue. You review, edit, approve, or reject. If you edit, it learns.

> An Instagram agent that watches your videos, reads your photos, and replies like you.

## Highlights

- **Context-aware drafting** — reads captions, comments, and visual cues from each post and Reel.
- **Your voice, not a template** — learns from your prior replies, tone, and vocabulary.
- **Approval-first workflow** — nothing posts until you approve it.
- **Learns from edits** — every change you make sharpens future drafts.
- **Instagram-native** — no scraping, no proxy hacks, no rate-limit games.

## How it works

1. **Connect** your Instagram account.
2. **ReplyMagic reads** each post — caption, comments, visuals, audio, and your past replies.
3. **Drafts appear** in an approval queue, written in your voice.
4. **You approve, edit, or reject.** Edits feed back into the model.
5. **Approved replies post** to Instagram. Everything else stays drafts.
